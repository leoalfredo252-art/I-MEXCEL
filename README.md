# I-MEXCEL

IMEXCEL is a global corporate, financial, and academic management platform developed by Leopoldino Alfredo. The solution integrates OCR, Restricted-Context Generative AI, and 2FA authentication to boost productivity, automate processes, and enhance security across corporate, independent, and educational environments.

IMEXCEL 🌐 2FA Corporate, Financial, and Academic Management Platform. Efficiency is not a destination; it is a tool. IMEXCEL was built to give time and control back to the user.

— Leopoldino Alfredo, Founder.

IMEXCEL is a software engineering solution designed to automate productivity and financial management. 

Focusing on Microsoft Fluent Design, the platform integrates context-restricted Generative AI and OCR tools to transform document chaos into structured data.

The 3 Pillars of IMEXCEL: Corporate: Conversion of physical reports into digital spreadsheets, featuring governance and hierarchical approval systems.

Self-Employment: Autonomous financial management that reads bank notifications and uses AI to provide advice on financial survival and savings.

Scholarship/Student: Intelligent monitoring of scholarships, tuition fees, and academic deadlines. 

Technologies Used Interface: Microsoft Fluent Design System. Processing: Generative AI (RAG) + High-Precision OCR. Security: SSO Authentication (Google) + Mandatory 2FA (Google Authenticator).

Export: Native conversion engine (.xlsx, .docx, .pptx, .pdf). 🛡 Security and Privacy (Zero-Retention Policy) We take privacy seriously. 

IMEXCEL operates under a Zero-Retention rule: no document images are permanently stored. Data is processed and deleted within 10 seconds, ensuring total confidentiality of user information.
